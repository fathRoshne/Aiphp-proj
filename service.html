Our Services

At BookCreating & Publishing, we offer a range of services to help you through every step of the publishing process. Whether you're just starting to write your book or you're ready to publish, we've got you covered.

  • Book Creation Tools and resources to help you write and format your book. Our platform offers easy-to-use templates and guidance to ensure your manuscript is polished and ready for publication.
  • Editing and Proofreading Professional editing and proofreading services to enhance the quality of your book. Our experienced editors will work with you to refine your manuscript and ensure it's error-free.
  • Cover Design Custom cover design services to create an eye-catching cover that represents your book. Our talented designers will work with you to bring your vision to life.
  • Publishing Assistance with ISBN registration, formatting, and printing. We handle the technical aspects of publishing so you can focus on your writing.
  • Digital Publishing Convert your book into an eBook and distribute it across major platforms like Amazon, Apple Books, and more. Reach a global audience with ease.
  • Marketing Strategies to promote your book and reach your target audience. We offer marketing plans, social media promotion, and more to help you build your author brand.
  • Distribution Get your book into bookstores and libraries. We work with major distributors to ensure your book is available in multiple formats and locations.
